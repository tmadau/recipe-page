Kumquat Pie recipe

It tastes as good as it is rare...

If you have never tasted this tiny fruit, you might be wondering what exactly this distinctive pie tastes like. The taste is a cross between a key lime pie, a lemon meringue pie, and a creamsicle.

ingredients:

  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (8 ounce) tub frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 2 eggs
  • ⅔ cup kumquat puree
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Steps:

  1. Step 1

    2. Beat condensed milk and whipped topping together in a bowl with an electric mixer. Add lemon juice and beat until thickened. Stir in kumquat puree. Pour into the pie crust.

  2. Step 2

    3. Freeze until firm, 2 to 3 hours. Let thaw for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
